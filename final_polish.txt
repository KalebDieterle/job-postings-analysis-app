============================================================
9. [CRITICAL BUG] ALL tested location detail pages display the WRONG city
   in the page header vs. the data. Examples:
   - /locations/new-york-ny       → page header says "Aberdeen, MD, US"
   - /locations/san-francisco-ca  → page header says "Adairsville, GA, US"

   by slug. This is a severe data mismatch affecting every location page.
